Fan Service

Bodysuit, cage skirt, shoulder piece, and bra custom created for Amanda Tori Meating for RuPaul's Drag Race Season 16. The bra features 3-D modeled and printed cage cups with working fan propellors inside. The shoulder pieces conceal 6 squirrel-cage fans and batteries to blow Amanda's hair around while she's wearing the look. As seen on Whatcha Packin Season 16!
