Worst Fear

Full look made for Tuna Melt's package for King of Drag Season 1. Based on creature design from the Taiwanese found footage horror film "Incantation". Costume set includes custom top and skirt wrap, CNC cut wooden back piece, 3D modeled crown and mask accessories, with hand sculpted details, and hand dyed and stenciled bodysuit.
